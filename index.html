About Barn Burner Relics

Founded by Nick Gatses in Manhattan, Illinois, Barn Burner Relics has grown into one of the most trusted names in the Native American artifact community. Nick's philosophy is simple: authenticity first, story always.

Every artifact that passes through his hands is meticulously documented — from find location and excavation context to collection history and cultural significance. He personally guarantees every piece he sells for life.

Nick is the host of the Collinsville Prehistoric Artifact Show and a regular exhibitor at the annual Starved Rock Native American Artifact Show — among the largest Native American artifact shows in the country — and his weekly Facebook auctions draw collectors from across the United States.

— Nick Gatses, Proprietor
