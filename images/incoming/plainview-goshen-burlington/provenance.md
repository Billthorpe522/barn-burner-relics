## Dickey evaluation (verbatim from COA)
> Plainview dates 8,000+ yrs. B.C. Goshen dates suggest a Plainview-Goshen complex could date as early as the Clovis culture. This is an unifaced lanceolate form occurring in the Great Plains with a proposed far East as the Mississippi River. Based on findings a proposed Southern tradition (Northern Plains) and Goshen-Plainview tradition (Southern Plains) has been proposed. The concept of Goshen and Plainview-Great Plains are likely to evolve once more data is available. The determination of Plainview vs. Goshen typology seems very subjective.
