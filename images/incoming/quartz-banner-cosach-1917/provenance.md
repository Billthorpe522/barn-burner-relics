## Notes
- Quartz is the rarest of all bannerstone materials — hard, brittle, difficult to work. Butterfly is the rarest of bannerstone forms. Both rarities in one piece is exceptional.
- Stone shows warm peach-pink coloration with subtle banding; not the typical translucent honey-amber quartz. The pink/peach variant is its own collector category.
- Biconical drilling indicates Archaic-era workmanship (the standard before conical drilling became dominant).
- Provenance depth is shallow by BBR standards — only what's physically on the piece is documented. No COA, no third-party authentication. Honest disclosure is appropriate here.
