## Notes
- Quartz is the rarest of all bannerstone materials — hard, brittle, difficult to work. Butterfly is the rarest of bannerstone forms. Pink/peach coloration is its own collector sub-category.
- Biconical drilling indicates Archaic-era workmanship (the standard before conical drilling became dominant).
- Provenance depth is real: three previous collectors, each documented on the piece itself (Cusick Collection, Mike Eck, Ex-Eckler/Owen).
- CUSICK naming convention follows the late-20th-century Midwestern collector sticker pattern.
