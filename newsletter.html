Template Structure Notes

  1. Header — brand mark + issue date
  2. Next-showcase bar — Monday date + 4 anchor times at a glance
  3. Nick's personal note — 2-3 sentences, human voice
  4. Last Monday's recap — hero hammers + night total + notable moment
  5. This Monday's heroes (×4) — time-stamped anchor schedule, each piece with provenance
  6. Hero section CTA — "Join the Group" button
  7. BIN spotlights (×3) — small image + price + link to Shopify (skip section if no BIN this week)
  8. Coming up (shows) — upcoming show dates (skip section if no upcoming shows)
  9. Collector's Spotlight — featured collector (optional, include when material exists)
  10. Subscribe CTA + footer — forwarding ask + standard newsletter plumbing
